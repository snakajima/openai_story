## CONSPIRACY PHASE (October-November 2023)

### October 2023

**Helen Toner Article Publication**
- **Event**: Board member Helen Toner publishes article praising Anthropic
- **Context**: Anthropic is OpenAI's rival, founded by Dario & Daniela Amodei
- **Sam's Reaction**: Furious, wants Helen removed from board
- **Ilya's Assessment** (Deposition p.306): "I thought it was not far from obviously inappropriate"
- **Discussion**: Ilya discussed with Sam about potentially removing Helen
- **Irony**: Ilya "at least at one point, expressed support" for removing Helen
- **Source**: Synopsis.md, Deposition p.305-306
- **Status**: ✓ CONFIRMED

**Critical Board Dynamics Shift**
- **Event**: "Board departures create an opening"
- **Result**: Independent directors now outnumber Sam's allies
- **Ilya's Recognition** (Deposition p.324-325): "I had been waiting for a moment when the board dynamics would allow for Altman to be replaced"
- **Source**: Synopsis.md (Act II), Deposition p.324, Wall Street Journal Exhibit 20
- **Specific Departures**: Not detailed in provided deposition pages
- **Status**: ✓ CONFIRMED timing, need details on which directors departed

**Board Composition After Departures**:
- Independent Directors: Helen Toner, Adam D'Angelo, Tasha McCauley
- Inside Directors: Ilya Sutskever, Greg Brockman
- Sam Altman (CEO, board member)

### Early November 2023 (First two weeks)

**Ilya Approaches Independent Directors**
- **Event**: Ilya approaches Helen Toner with concerns
- **Helen's Action**: Brings in Adam D'Angelo and Tasha McCauley
- **Request**: They ask for evidence
- **Source**: Synopsis.md (Act III)
- **Status**: Implied by deposition, not explicitly dated

**The 52-Page Memo Compilation**
- **Event**: Ilya works frantically to compile evidence memo
- **Commissioner**: Adam D'Angelo (most likely) asked for screenshots
- **Method**: Disappearing emails (ephemeral messages)
- **Reason for Secrecy** (Deposition p.128, 131): "I was worried that those memos will somehow leak" / "had he [Sam] become aware of these discussions, he would just find a way to make them disappear"
- **Content**:
  - Allegations of Sam's pattern of lying and undermining
  - Screenshots from Mira
  - Claims about Sam's firing from Y Combinator
  - Claims about Greg being fired from Stripe
- **Source**: Deposition p.128-135, Exhibit 19
- **Key Quote** (Deposition p.129): "I was pretty careful. Most of the screenshots that I have... I get them from Mira Murati."
- **Status**: ✓ CONFIRMED

**Memo Distribution**:
- **Recipients**: Independent directors ONLY (Helen, Adam, Tasha)
- **Excluded**: Sam Altman, Greg Brockman
- **Format**: Sent via disappearing email
- **First Page Summary**: "Sam exhibits a consistent pattern of lying, undermining his execs, and pitting his execs against one another"

**Verification Issues** (Deposition p.310-313):
- Ilya did NOT speak to Brad Lightcap about YC claims
- Ilya did NOT speak to Jason Kwon about DSB/Turbo matter
- Ilya did NOT speak to Greg Brockman about Stripe claims
- Ilya did NOT speak to Peter Welinder (suggested witness)
- Ilya did NOT speak to Nick Ryder (suggested witness)
- Ilya did NOT speak to Bob McGrew (suggested witness)
- **Ilya's Admission** (p.310): "I fully believed the information that Mira was giving me"
- **Ilya's Later Reflection** (p.312): "I've learned the critical importance of firsthand knowledge for matters like this"

**Secret Board Meetings**
- **Event**: Independent directors meet secretly
- **Attendance**: Sometimes without Sam and Greg present
- **Paranoia**: Worried about leaks
- **Purpose**: Discussing Ilya's memo and allegations
- **Source**: Synopsis.md (Act III)
- **Note**: Specific meeting dates not provided in deposition

**Board Member Engagement** (Deposition p.301-303):
- **Helen Toner**: Lives in D.C. (at least part time), not always physically present
- **Tasha McCauley**: Not always physically present at meetings
- **Attendance Pattern**: Both attended "from time to time" physically, sometimes remote
- **Ilya's Assessment** (p.303): "They seemed to have some familiarity [with OpenAI operations], but it's hard for me to assess"
- **Board Experience** (p.301): "The process was rushed because the board was inexperienced" in "board matters"

### November 17, 2023 (Friday)

**The Day Before: Final Board Meeting**
- **Event**: Secret board meeting - decision is made
- **Decision**: Termination of Sam Altman
- **Ilya's Certainty** (Deposition p.131): "Termination" when asked what action was appropriate
- **Plan**: "Move fast, announce publicly before Sam can counter"
- **Ilya's Expectation** (Deposition p.326): Did not expect employees to cheer, but "have not expected them to feel strongly either way"

- **Status**: ✓ Date confirmed

## CRISIS WEEKEND (November 18-20, 2023)

### November 18, 2023 (Saturday) - THE FIRING

**Morning/Midday: The Termination**
- **Event**: Sam Altman called into video meeting, told he's terminated
- **Method**: Video call (likely Zoom/similar)
- **Simultaneous**: Public announcement released
- **Reaction**: Silicon Valley in shock
- **Source**: Synopsis.md (Act IV)
- **Status**: ✓ CONFIRMED (widely reported)

**Greg Brockman's Status**:
- Also removed from board (timing relative to Sam unclear in deposition)

**Immediate Aftermath (Saturday Afternoon/Evening)**
- **Employee Reaction**: Immediate revolt
- **Threat**: Mass resignation
- **Microsoft's Move**: Offers to hire entire OpenAI team
- **Ilya's Surprise**: Expected neutral reaction, got strong opposition
- **Source**: Synopsis.md (Act V), Deposition p.326

**Anthropic Merger Discussions Begin**
- **Event**: Anthropic reaches out about potential merger
- **When**: "On Saturday" per Ilya (Deposition p.319)
- **Initial Contact**: Either Helen to Anthropic or Anthropic to Helen (Ilya unsure which)
- **Source**: Deposition p.319-321
- **Status**: ✓ CONFIRMED

### November 18-19, 2023 (Saturday-Sunday) - MERGER TALKS

**Board Call About Anthropic Proposal**
- **Event**: Helen tells other board members about Anthropic outreach
- **Participants**: Helen and "the other board members" (independent directors)
- **Source**: Deposition p.320

**Call with Anthropic Leadership**
- **Event**: Board call with Anthropic executives
- **Anthropic Participants**:
  - Dario Amodei (CEO)
  - Daniela Amodei (President)
  - At least one other person (possibly more)
- **Proposal**: Anthropic to merge with OpenAI and "take over its leadership"
- **Anthropic's Tone**: "Expressing their excitement"
- **Practical Issues**: "Expressing the issue -- the practical challenges that they would have with it"
- **Timing**: "Either on Saturday or on Sunday. It was not on Monday" (Deposition p.320)
- **Source**: Deposition p.319-321
- **Status**: ✓ CONFIRMED

**Ilya's Reaction to Merger**:
- **Quote** (p.320): "I was very unhappy about it"
- **Quote** (p.320): "Because I really did not want OpenAI to merge with Anthropic"
- **Quote** (p.320): "I just didn't want to"

**Other Board Members' Reactions**:
- **Quote** (p.321): "They were a lot more supportive, yes"
- **Specific**: "At the very least, none were unsupportive"
- **Most Supportive**: "My recollection is that Helen was the most supportive"

**Merger Outcome**:
- **Result**: Proposal did not continue
- **Reason** (p.321): "There were some practical obstacles that Anthropic has raised"
- **Duration**: "Extremely briefly"
- **Ilya's Knowledge of Obstacles**: "No" - doesn't know what they were

**Helen Toner's Statement on Mission**:
- **Event**: Meeting with board members and executive team
- **Context**: Executives said if Sam doesn't return, OpenAI will be destroyed and that's inconsistent with mission
- **Helen's Response** (Deposition p.307): "Something to the effect of that it is consistent" - i.e., destroying OpenAI would be consistent with the mission
- **Ilya's Testimony** (p.307): "I think she said it even more directly than that"
- **Source**: Deposition p.307
- **Status**: ✓ CONFIRMED
- **Note**: This shocked many, suggesting mission purity over company survival

### November 19-20, 2023 (Sunday-Monday) - THE COLLAPSE

**Employee Pressure Intensifies**
- **Event**: Employees demand the board resign
- **Threat**: Mass resignation continues
- **Microsoft Alternative**: Employees have offer to move en masse
- **Source**: Synopsis.md (Act V)

**Board Realization**
- **Event**: Board realizes "they've miscalculated catastrophically"
- **Source**: Synopsis.md (Act V)

**Ilya's Tweet**
- **Event**: Ilya tweets his regret about the decision
- **Significance**: Public break from board position
- **Source**: Synopsis.md (Act V)
- **Status**: Historical record (tweet was public)

### November 21-22, 2023 (Tuesday-Wednesday) - REVERSAL

**Approximate: Within 5 Days of Firing**
- **Event**: Sam Altman reinstated as CEO
- **Event**: Board members resign (Helen Toner, Adam D'Angelo, Tasha McCauley)
- **New Board**: Reconstituted with different members
- **Source**: Synopsis.md (Act V)
- **Status**: ✓ CONFIRMED (widely reported)
