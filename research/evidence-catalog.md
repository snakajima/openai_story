**Type**: Internal memorandum with screenshots
**Author**: Ilya Sutskever

**Pages**: 52 pages (exhibits pages 528-579 referenced in transcript)
**Recipients**: Independent directors only (Adam D'Angelo, Helen Toner, Tasha McCauley)

**Status**:

- ⚠️ HIGHLY CONFIDENTIAL - Attorneys' eyes only designation
- ⚠️ Special handling - Copies must be returned per party agreement

**Deposition Citations**: pp.128-135, 308-318

**Content Summary**:
- **First page**: "Sam exhibits a consistent pattern of lying, undermining his execs, and pitting his execs against one another" (p.130)
- **Multiple sections** documenting alleged misconduct patterns
- **Screenshots throughout** - primarily from Mira Murati

**Key Sections Identified**:

1. **"Sam was pushed out from YC"** (p.529)
   - Alleges similar behaviors to current concerns
   - Source: Conversation Mira had with Brad Lightcap (secondhand)
   - Quote: "He was creating chaos, starting lots of new projects, pitting people against each other" (p.309)

2. **Greg Brockman's departure from Stripe** (p.531)
   - Allegation: "Greg was essentially fired from Stripe as well" (p.310)
   - Source: Mira Murati (secondhand - NOT verified with Greg)

3. **"Lying to Mira About Jason's Opinion About the DSB"** (p.531)
   - Screenshots of communications re: GPT-4 Turbo
   - Jason Kwon referenced
   - Source: Screenshots from Mira Murati
   - NOT verified with Jason Kwon (p.311)

4. **"Pitting People Against Each Other"** (p.540-542)
   - Subsection: "Daniela versus Mira" - Daniela Amodei vs Mira Murati
   - Subsection: "Dario versus Greg, Ilya" - Dario Amodei wanting to run research/fire Greg
   - Source: Mira Murati (p.314)

5. **"The Jakub Story"** (p.548)
   - "Involves Sam lying, undermining Mira, undermining Ilya, and pitting Jakub against Ilya. Joint work with Greg and Jakub" (p.316)
   - Allegation: Sam told conflicting things about how company would be run

6. **"Subtle Retaliation in Response to Mira's Feedback"** (p.564)
   - Diane Yoon meetings with Mira and Sam
   - NOT verified with Diane Yoon (p.318)

7. **Mira's Performance Review of Sam** (pp.565-566)
   - Screenshot of review
   - Source: Mira Murati

8. **Text Messages Between Greg Brockman and Sam Altman** (p.570)
   - Source: Mira Murati (p.318)

**Witnesses Suggested for Follow-up** (within memo):
- Bob McGrew (p.313)
- Nick Ryder (p.313)
- Peter Welinder (p.315, p.542)
- **Status**: None interviewed by board before action (p.313)

**Critical Limitation Admitted**:
> "I've learned the critical importance of firsthand knowledge for matters like this" (p.312)
- Nearly all evidence was secondhand from Mira Murati
- No verification with subjects (Brad Lightcap, Greg Brockman, Jason Kwon, Diane Yoon, Jakub)

**Dramatic Potential**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Central document of the entire crisis
- Physical manifestation of Ilya's case
- Shows methodology (secondhand evidence, no verification)
- Disappeared/ephemeral transmission suggests consciousness of sensitivity
- Could be recreated on-screen with graphics, screenshots

## XIV. KEY EVIDENTIARY THEMES

### THEME 1: SECONDHAND EVIDENCE
Almost every allegation in Exhibit 19 came from Mira Murati, never verified firsthand. This is the central methodological flaw.

### THEME 2: EPHEMERAL COMMUNICATION
Use of disappearing emails shows:
- Consciousness of sensitivity
- Fear of leaks
- Intent to avoid permanent record

### THEME 3: INCOMPLETE PROCESS
Suggested witnesses never interviewed, allegations never verified, process "rushed because the board was inexperienced" (p.301)

### THEME 4: HIDDEN CONNECTIONS
- Helen Toner → Open Philanthropy → Holden Karnofsky → Daniela Amodei → Anthropic
- Network of relationships influenced board dynamics and Anthropic merger discussions

### THEME 5: DOCUMENTS AS DRAMA
Physical documents become characters: the memo, the screenshots, the missing Brockman memo, the texts. Can be shown on screen dramatically.
